THE BOOK OF ELI

The message of The Book of Eli is about the power of faith, the importance of preserving knowledge, and the struggle for hope in a bleak world. The film explores how faith can provide meaning and purpose, even in the face of despair, and how knowledge, particularly religious texts, can be both a source of hope and a tool for control. Ultimately, the story suggests that having faith is essential for rebuilding a better future
